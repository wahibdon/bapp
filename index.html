HOW TO ENTER

Here's how to submit your photo for a chance to win:

  • Snap a selfie of you and a Polar Pop cup.
  • Share your selfie by clicking the Submit an Entry button.
  • You may also enter the contest by posting your picture on Instagram with the hashtag #PolarPopCup.
  • Daily Prize winners will receive a cup of Polar Pop FREE every day for a year.
  • One Grand Prize winner will be randomly selected at the end of the promotion period. For more information, see the Official Rules here
  • Contestants may enter as often as they like, however each photograph must be original, duplicates will be considered as one entry.
